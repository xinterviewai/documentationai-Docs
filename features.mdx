## Craft Your Interview Questions

Create questions that reveal candidate fit. You select from templates or build custom ones, setting time limits and required responses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Template" icon="book-open">
    Start with role-specific templates like "Software Engineer" or "Sales Lead".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Prompts" icon="edit">
    Add your questions. Example: "Describe how you handled a difficult client."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Parameters" icon="settings">
    Configure response time (e.g., 2 minutes) and video quality requirements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use open-ended questions to assess soft skills alongside technical ones.
</Callout>

## Manage Candidate Outreach and Responses

Invite candidates seamlessly and track their submissions.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Invite" icon="mail">
    Upload candidate emails and send personalized invites from your dashboard at `https://app.xinterview.ai`.
    
    ```
    Subject: Video Interview for [Role] at [Company]
    
    Hi [Name],
    
    Complete your 3-question video interview here: [Unique Link]
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Share Link" icon="link">
    Generate a shareable link for job boards or messaging apps.
    
    Example link: `https://app.xinterview.ai/interview/abc123`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bulk Upload" icon="upload">
    Import CSV with emails and names for mass invites.
    
    ```
    name,email
    John Doe,john@example.com
    Jane Smith,jane@example.com
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## AI Insights for Better Decisions

Xinterview AI analyzes responses automatically, highlighting key traits.

| Insight Type | Description | Benefit |
|--------------|-------------|---------|
| Sentiment Analysis | Detects enthusiasm and confidence | Identifies cultural fit |
| Keyword Matching | Matches skills from your job description | Speeds technical screening |
| Consistency Check | Flags contradictory answers | Reduces bias in reviews |
| Ranking Score | Auto-ranks top candidates | Prioritizes interviews |

<Callout kind="success">
  AI insights integrate with your ATS via webhooks at `https://your-webhook-url.com/xinterview`.
</Callout>
