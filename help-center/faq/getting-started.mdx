## Getting Started

Sign up and set up your first interview fast.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://dashboard.xinterview.ai/register`
2. Fill in your basic details
3. Choose a plan (free trial available)
4. Create your first project
5. Send interview invites to candidates

<Callout kind="tip">
Use your dashboard to track candidate responses.
</Callout>

For more, visit the guides section.
