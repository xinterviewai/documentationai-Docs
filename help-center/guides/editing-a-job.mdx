## Edit a job

Use the Dashboard to update an existing job listing, adjust the interview process, and manage who can work on the role.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Active Jobs">
    Go to your Dashboard and find the job under **Active Jobs**.

    Click **Edit Job** to open the job editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit job details">
    Update the core job information, including the **position**, **deadline**, **time zone**, and **description**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage interview questions">
    Add, edit, or delete interview questions.

    You can also adjust **takes**, **thinking time**, and **allocated time** for each question.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add or remove team members">
    Update the team members who can access or manage the job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite candidates">
    Add candidate details and send invitations from the job editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** at the bottom of the page when you are done.

    If you need help, contact support.
  </Step>
</Steps>
