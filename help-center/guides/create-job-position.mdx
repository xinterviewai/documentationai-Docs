## Create your job position

Add a job position before you start building interview questions. This gives your interview a clear title, deadline, language, timezone, and description.

<Callout kind="info">

Make sure you are signed in to your Xinterview AI dashboard before you begin.

</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Sign in to Xinterview AI and go to the dashboard where you manage interviews.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a job position">
    Select the option to add a new job from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the job details">
    Enter the required fields:

    - **Job Title** — Use the position name that candidates will see.
    - **Deadline** — Set the date by which the interview should be completed.
    - **Language** — Choose the language candidates will use during the interview.
    - **Timezone** — Set the timezone that applies to the deadline and scheduling.
    - **Job Description** — Add the role description.

    You can generate the job description with AI or paste it in manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the job">
    Save the new job position. The dashboard should now show the job as added.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your job position is now ready. Next, move on to crafting your video interview questions.
