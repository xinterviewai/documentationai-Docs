## Add and invite candidates

Use this workflow to add candidates to an interview and send invitations in the way that fits your hiring process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an invite link">

  Generate an invite link when you want to share one URL with multiple candidates or post the link in a recruiting channel.

  Turn on CV collection if you want candidates to upload a CV before they start the interview. Leave it off if you want the link to go straight to the interview flow.

  <Callout kind="info">

  Use the invite link when you want a reusable invitation. If you enable CV collection, candidates must submit their CV before they continue.

  </Callout>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite candidates by email">

  Send invitations directly to specific candidates when you already have their email addresses.

  Add each candidate’s email address, review the invitation details, and send the invite. Each candidate receives an individual invitation to the interview.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Bulk invite candidates">

  Use bulk invite when you need to add many candidates at once.

  Upload a CSV file with the required candidate details, then import the file to create and send the invitations in one action. Make sure your CSV matches the format required by your interview setup before you upload it.

  <Callout kind="info">

  For bulk invite, upload a CSV file that includes the candidate details required by your interview flow.

  </Callout>

  </Step>
</Steps>
