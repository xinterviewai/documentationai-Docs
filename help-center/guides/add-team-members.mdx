## Add team members to an interview process

Use team members to involve the right people in your interview process. You can add a member, set their notification preferences, and save the update in one workflow.

<Callout kind="info">

If your team uses email notifications, make sure SMTP is configured before you add members who should receive email updates.

</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the interview process">

  Go to the interview process where you want to add teammates. Open the team members section from the process settings.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a team member">

  Choose the person you want to add from the available team members list.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure email notifications">

  Turn on email notifications for the member if they need to receive updates about the interview process.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">

  Save the interview process to apply the new team member settings.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

After you save, the selected team member appears in the interview process with the notification settings you chose. If email notifications are enabled, the member receives updates through the configured SMTP setup.
