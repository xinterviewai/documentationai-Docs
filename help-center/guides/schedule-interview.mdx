## Schedule Your Interview

Follow these steps to set up an interview session.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Log in at `dashboard.xinterview.ai` and choose your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Interview">
    Click "New Interview" and set the title & instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Questions">
    Add your video or text questions for candidates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invites">
    Enter candidate emails and send the invite.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your candidates will receive a link to record responses.
