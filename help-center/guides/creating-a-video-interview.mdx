## Create your video interview

Use this workflow to build a video interview from start to finish.

![Temporary interview setup flow placeholder](https://placehold.co/1200x675/png?text=Replace+this+image+later)

<Callout kind="info">

Use this page as a quick setup checklist. Each step adds the pieces you need before you invite candidates.

</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add the job position">
    Create the job first, then enter the title, deadline, timezone, and an optional description. You can paste a job description or use AI to help generate it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the video interview">
    Choose AI-generated questions, start from a template, or write custom questions. Set the allowed takes, thinking time, and allocated time for each response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add team members">
    Add the people who need access to the job so they can review the interview and collaborate on hiring decisions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the candidate experience">
    Adjust the look and feel to match your brand so candidates see a consistent experience from the first screen to the last.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite candidates">
    Share the interview by invite link, email, or bulk CSV upload, depending on how many candidates you need to reach.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have created the foundation for a complete video interview. Nice work!
