---
title: Video Upload Error
description: Fix issues with candidate video uploads.
---

## Symptoms

- Candidate sees error when uploading video
- Message: "Video upload failed"

## Possible Causes

- File too large or wrong format
- Poor internet connection

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check file size & format">
    Ensure video is under 200MB and in MP4 format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test your connection">
    Recommend candidate uses a fast, stable network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry">
    Reload the page and re-upload the video.
  </Step>
</Steps>
