## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A valid email address for registration
- Company details for your profile setup
- Candidate email list or names for testing invites

</Callout>

No credit card is required for the 14-day free trial.

## Sign Up for Your Free Trial

Get started in under 2 minutes by creating your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Registration Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to the [sign-up page](https://app.xinterview.ai/register/) and enter your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile Setup" icon="user">
    Provide your company name, role, and team size. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="dashboard">
    Log in at [https://app.xinterview.ai/login/](https://app.xinterview.ai/login/) to reach your personalized dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! You now have access to all core features during your trial.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Interview Setup

Build a custom video interview tailored to your hiring needs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Craft Questions" icon="edit-3">
    From the dashboard, click `New Interview`. Add 3-5 key questions like "Describe your experience with React." Set duration to 5-10 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings" icon="settings">
    Choose asynchronous video responses, enable AI scoring, and select notification preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Preview" icon="play">
    Preview the candidate experience and save your setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invite Candidates

Share one-click invite links effortlessly across methods.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Invite" icon="mail">
    Copy the generated invite link and send it directly via your email client.

    ```
    Subject: Complete Your Video Interview for [Job Title]

    Hi [Candidate Name],

    Please complete this short video interview: https://app.xinterview.ai/interview/YOUR_INVITE_ID

    Best,
    Your Team
    ```

    Candidates receive automated reminders.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Shareable Link" icon="link">
    Post the link in job descriptions, Slack, or LinkedIn. Track opens and completions in real-time from your dashboard.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bulk Upload" icon="upload">
    Upload a CSV with candidate emails.

    ```csv
    email,name,job_title
    candidate@example.com,John Doe,Frontend Developer
    jane@company.com,Jane Smith,Backend Engineer
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Dashboard Navigation Basics

Your dashboard centralizes everything:

- **Interviews Tab**: View active setups and responses
- **Candidates Tab**: Manage invites and scores
- **Analytics Tab**: Track completion rates and AI insights
- **Settings**: Update profile and billing

Use the search bar to quickly find interviews by ID or candidate name.
