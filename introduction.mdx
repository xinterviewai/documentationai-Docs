## Welcome to Xinterview

Xinterview is an AI video-interview platform built to help hiring teams move faster, stay aligned, and make better decisions.

<Callout kind="info">
  Think of this as your launch pad: set up your space, create a role, invite candidates, and review interviews in one smooth flow.
</Callout>

## What you'll learn

- Set up your account and brand it for your team
- Create a job position and shape the interview
- Invite candidates through flexible methods
- Review, compare, and share interview responses

## Your hiring journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your account">
    Make Xinterview feel like your own workspace.

    - Add your branding and logo
    - Invite team members who will take part in hiring
    - Upload a profile picture for a polished account
    - Logo guidance: horizontal logo 180 px × 90 px, vertical logo 180 px × 180 px
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a job position">
    Turn a role into a structured interview experience.

    - Add the job
    - Create the video interview questions
    - Add the right team members for the position
    - Customize branding colors and other visual details
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite candidates">
    Reach candidates in the way that works best for your workflow.

    - Invite link for quick sharing
    - Email invites for personal outreach
    - Bulk CSV invites for larger candidate lists
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and share interviews">
    Once interviews are complete, your team can move from screening to decision-making.

    - Review candidate responses
    - Share interviews internally for comparison
    - Keep the hiring process moving with clearer context
  </Step>
</Steps>
